How you can contribute

There are a few ways you can contribute to Code And Talk.

On GitHub

The most direct way to contribute is to join us on GitHub, and do one or more of the following:

  • Tackle open Issues; and
  • Submit data on upcoming conferences, podcasts you enjoy, and speakers who you follow by submitting pull requests.

Submit data

You can also submit event, podcast, and speaker information directly to us by sending us an email.
