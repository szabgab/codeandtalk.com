How you can contribute

There are a few ways you can contribute to Code And Talk.

On GitHub

The most direct way to contribute is to join us on GitHub, and either:

  • Tackle open Issues; or
  • Submit data on upcoming conferences, podcasts you enjoy, and speakers who you follow by submitting pull requests (or both).

Submit data

You can also add events, submit interesting podcasts and videos, as well as information about people worth following directly to us by sending Gabor an email.
